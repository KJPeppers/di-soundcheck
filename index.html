WELCOME TO YOUR DI SOUNDCHECK

Every team has a rhythm. The way you collaborate, plan, and adapt to change creates a unique sound—your approach to Design Intelligence® in action.

We want to understand how DI is working for you, your team, and your clients. This isn't a test. There are no right or wrong answers—just honest reflections on how your team actually works.

You'll answer 18 questions about your team's practices. At the end, you'll discover your team's unique sound and see where DI is hitting strongest.

Things to keep in mind as you proceed:

  1. Your responses are confidential. Results will be aggregated with your team
  2. Select what's MOST true. For each question, more than one option may represent your team. Choose the option your team would prioritize most regularly
  3. The whole thing will take about 10-15 minutes
